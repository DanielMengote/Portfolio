About me

Developer & Designer

Hello, my name is Daniel Mengote and I am writing to introduce myself. My interests include photography, art, and music. I enjoy making things out of clay and woodworking. I am a native of the Philippines and have been here for over 20 years now. I grew up in Borongan City where I attended school. After high school, I moved to Antipolo where I went to college at Cogee College.
